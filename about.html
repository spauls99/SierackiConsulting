Who We Are

About Sieracki Consulting

GIS Expertise Rooted in the Landscape

Sieracki Consulting is an independent GIS and spatial research firm based in the Pacific Northwest. We combine technical GIS skills with a deep understanding of environmental and land use issues to produce analysis that is accurate, clear, and actionable.

Our work spans environmental research, land use analysis, terrain modeling, hydrologic analysis, and habitat mapping. We work with a range of clients including land managers, conservation organizations, engineering firms, and public agencies.

Every project begins with careful listening — understanding what decisions the map needs to support and what questions the data needs to answer. From there we design an analysis approach that gets to the right answer efficiently.

Approach

Good spatial analysis requires both technical precision and contextual understanding. We bring both. Our workflow integrates industry-standard GIS tools (Esri ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Python/GeoPandas) with field knowledge and a commitment to delivering results that hold up to scrutiny.

We believe in transparent methodology — every deliverable comes with clear documentation of data sources, analytical methods, and limitations. You should be able to replicate or audit our work.

About Sieracki Consulting

GIS Expertise Rooted in the Landscape

Sieracki Consulting is an independent GIS and spatial research practice based in the Pacific Northwest. I combine technical GIS skills with a deep understanding of environmental and land use issues to produce quality maps and defensible analysis that are accurate, clear, and actionable.

My work spans environmental research, land use analysis, terrain modeling, hydrologic analysis, photo documentation,and habitat mapping. I work with a range of clients including land managers, conservation organizations, and engineering firms.

Every project begins with careful listening ----- understanding what decisions the map needs to support and what questions the data needs to answer. From there I design an analysis approach that gets to the right answer efficiently.

Approach

Good spatial analysis requires both technical precision and contextual understanding. The workflow integrates industry-standard GIS tools with field knowledge and a commitment to delivering results that hold up to scrutiny.

I believe in transparent methodology — every deliverable comes with clear documentation of data sources, analytical methods, and limitations. You should be able to replicate or audit the work.

Project Commenting

I provide expert spatial analysis and mapping to support formal project comments at all levels of government review. Our commenting services cover:

  • Local Projects: Site plans, zoning changes, and development proposals requiring local permitting
  • State Projects: Transportation improvement and state environmental reviews
  • NEPA Projects: Federal actions requiring Environmental Impact Statements (EIS) or Environmental Assessments (EA)

Each comment submission includes detailed spatial analysis, data-driven recommendations, and clear visual documentation to strengthen your position in the regulatory process.

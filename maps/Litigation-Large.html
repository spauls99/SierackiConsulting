Relation to Grizzly Bear Recovery Areas, Colville National Forest, Washington, USA.

Data Source USFS Timber Sale Records
Analysis Type Litigation / Grizzly Bear Recovery Areas
Region Colville National Forest

About This Map

This litigation support map frames the project area in relation to designated grizzly bear recovery areas and the BORZ boundary. It synthesizes ongoing and potential logging units with recovery area overlays to clarify potential impacts on recovery objectives and listed species conservation.

The spatial analysis highlights where project features intersect grizzly recovery areas and BORZ management zones, providing a fact-based foundation for legal comment, impact assessment, and mitigation planning. The map emphasizes simplicity and clarity to ensure key spatial relationships are immediately apparent and defensible in regulatory proceedings.

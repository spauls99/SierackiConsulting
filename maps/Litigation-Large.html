Litigation Map Example

Potential Old Growth Map, Colville National Forest, Washington, USA.

Data Source USFS Timber Sale Records
Analysis Type Litigation / Potential Old Growth
Region Colville National Forest

About This Map

This litigation support map was created for environmental impact assessment and legal proceedings related to old growth forest protection. The analysis combines timber sale records with forest inventory data to identify areas of potential old growth that may be affected by logging activities.

The map shows timber sale boundaries overlaid with forest age class data, lidar analysis was used to highlight potential areas where old growth characteristics may exist. This spatial analysis was used to support litigation efforts protecting remaining old growth forests in the Colville National Forest. Legend removed.

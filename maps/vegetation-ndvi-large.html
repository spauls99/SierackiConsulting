About This Map

NDVI was derived from near-infrared and red bands of Sentinel-2 imagery composited over the summer growing season to minimize cloud contamination. Values range from -1 to 1, with higher values indicating denser, healthier vegetation. The output was used to characterize vegetation productivity across a research watershed and identify areas of potential stress or disturbance.

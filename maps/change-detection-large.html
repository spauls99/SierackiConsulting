About This Map

Classified land cover layers from 2010 and 2024 were compared using a cross-tabulation matrix to quantify transitions between cover types. Forest loss, wetland conversion, and expansion of shrub cover are highlighted as primary change categories. Results were validated against aerial photography and field data collected at a stratified random sample of change locations.

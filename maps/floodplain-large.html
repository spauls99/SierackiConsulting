About This Map

FEMA 100-year floodplain boundaries were intersected with riparian buffer polygons (50m and 100m) derived from the National Hydrography Dataset stream network. The analysis was overlaid with land ownership data to identify parcels with high conservation value — those containing floodplain and riparian habitat not currently under conservation easement. Results informed a conservation prioritization scoring model.

About This Map

A maximum likelihood supervised classification was applied to 10-meter Sentinel-2 multispectral imagery using training samples collected from field visits and aerial photo interpretation. Classes include conifer forest, deciduous forest, shrub/scrub, herbaceous, wetland, agriculture, and developed land. Overall accuracy was assessed using a confusion matrix with independent validation points.

About This Map

SSURGO soil polygons were joined to their component-level attributes (drainage class, texture, available water capacity) and displayed alongside bedrock geology units from the state geological survey. The combined layer was used to characterize soil-forming factors across a research watershed, supporting a study of soil carbon storage and forest productivity relationships.

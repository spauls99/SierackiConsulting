About This Map

Sub-watershed boundaries were delineated using the Arc Hydro toolset applied to a conditioned 10-meter DEM. Flow direction and accumulation grids were calculated, and pour points were placed at stream junctions to define sub-watershed polygons. Streams were classified by Strahler order. The resulting dataset was used to prioritize tributary watersheds for restoration planning.
